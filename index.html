Artur Toshev

I'm a PhD Candidate at Technical University of Munich where I am supervised by Professor Nikolaus Adams. My research combines Machine Learning and Particle-Based Fluid Dynamics.

More about me can be found in my CV. If you have some questions, just drop me an email or connect with me on one of the following platforms.

Email /  Twitter  /  Linkedin  /  Github

Research

I'm interested in Geometric DL, Physics-based ML, Differentiable Programming (DP), turbulence modelling, and Uncertainty Quantification (UQ). The current goal of my research is to speed-up conventional Smoothed-Particle Hydrodynamics (SPH) with ML.

Toshev Colloquium
Thanks to the still ongoing pandemic, which forced our global society to enhance the use of web-based communication tools, me and a few fellow students grasped the opportunity. Early 2021, we started to regularly meet and discuss our own research, current trends in computer science, engineering and physics, as well as some socio-political trends.
If you want to take part in our discussions and/or present something yourself, just write me an Email.
